Modified PolypSegNet for Polyp-Segmentation of Colonoscopy Images

Published at ICAAAIML-2023: International Conference on Advances and Applications of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Springer

The research paper introduces a modified PolypSegNet architecture that enhances polyp segmentation in colonoscopy images by integrating a squeeze and excitation module with a feature pyramid network. Trained on the Kvasir-SEG dataset, the model improves localization and boundary demarcation, facilitating real-time, accurate detection for better gastrointestinal diagnostics.

A Brief Review on Artificial Neural Network : Network Structures and Applications

Published at International Conference on Advanced Computing and Communication Systems (ICACCS), IEEE

The research paper explores artificial neural networks (ANN), covering their structure, learning mechanisms, and applications in fields like medicine and transportation. It highlights the evolution of ANNs and their effectiveness in tasks such as breast cancer diagnosis, ECG analysis, and traffic pattern recognition. The paper underscores the practical significance of ANNs in real-world problem-solving.
